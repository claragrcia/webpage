About Clara

Clara Garcia is a bilingual multimedia journalist + news producer. She is a recent graduate from the University of Florida studying Telecommunications-News with a certificate in Latin American studies.

Born and raised in Maracaibo, Venezuela, Clara became interested in storytelling, history, and politics from a young age. She worked as a reporter and news producer for PBS-affiliate WUFT, as well as a bilingual reporter for Noticias WUFT. Previously, she served as the anchor and executive producer of Noticias WUFT.

Other than journalism, Clara is passionate about traveling, coffee, world politics, arepas, latin music & a little bit of code.
